# ❄️ Arctic Survivor

![Arctic Survivor](assets/arctic-survivalist.png)

## Overview

A hardened explorer who has spent decades surviving in the most unforgiving frozen environments on Earth. This example demonstrates environmental storytelling, survival realism, and extreme-weather character design.
