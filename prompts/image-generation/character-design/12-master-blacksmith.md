# 🔥 Master Blacksmith

![Master Blacksmith](assets/volcano-blacksmith.png)

## Overview

A legendary forge master renowned across kingdoms for crafting weapons and armor worthy of heroes and kings. This example demonstrates craftsmanship-focused character design, dramatic lighting, and environmental storytelling through fire, steel, and industry.
