# 🌲 Forest Ranger

![Forest Ranger](assets/forest-ranger.png)

## Overview

A skilled protector of ancient woodlands, dedicated to preserving nature and defending the balance between civilization and the wild. This example demonstrates environmental storytelling, fantasy realism, and nature-focused character design.
