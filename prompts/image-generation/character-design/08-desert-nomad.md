# 🏜️ Desert Nomad

![Desert Nomad](assets/desert-nomad.png)

## Overview

A legendary traveler who guides caravans across endless seas of sand, surviving harsh environments through experience, resilience, and ancient knowledge. This example demonstrates adventure storytelling, environmental character design, and cinematic travel aesthetics.
