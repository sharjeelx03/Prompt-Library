# 🐉 Dragon Rider

![Dragon Rider](assets/dragon-rider.png)

## Overview

A legendary warrior bonded to one of the last ancient dragons. Together they patrol the skies, defend forgotten kingdoms, and preserve the balance between humanity and mythical creatures. This example demonstrates epic fantasy storytelling, heroic character design, and large-scale cinematic composition.
