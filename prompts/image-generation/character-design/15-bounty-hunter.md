# 🎯 Bounty Hunter

![Bounty Hunter](assets/whiskers-and-nibbles.png)

## Overview

A notorious bounty hunter known across star systems for tracking the galaxy's most dangerous fugitives. This example demonstrates sci-fi western aesthetics, rugged character design, and action-oriented visual storytelling.
