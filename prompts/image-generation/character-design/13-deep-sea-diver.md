# 🌊 Deep Sea Diver

![Deep Sea Diver](assets/deep-sea-diver.png)

## Overview

A fearless marine explorer venturing into the darkest depths of the ocean in search of forgotten civilizations and undiscovered lifeforms. This example demonstrates underwater storytelling, environmental atmosphere, and exploration-focused character design.
